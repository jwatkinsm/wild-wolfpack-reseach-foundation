About Us

The Wild Wolf-Pack Research Foundation (WWPRF) is an nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization that is dediacated to the study and conservation of wolves.

Research

WWPRF participates primarily in behavoral and cognitive research. Past research projects have included investigations into howling, scent rolling, reproductive behavior,aggression, rank order, human interaction time lengths, pointing, opening apparatuses and feeding patterns

Conservation

The WWPRF is an sanctuary to specific species wolves the Red wolf and gray wolf that are found in Virginia and North Coralina. The Red wolf is is one of the most endangered species in North America.

Donations

All Donations go to the needs of the wolves to support and enrich their lives such as food and veterinarian care as well as care and maitence for sanctuary.
